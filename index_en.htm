The Pangloss Collection

 

This public Archive contains over 2,300 recordings (over 400  hours) in more than 130 languages, including 900 documents that are transcribed and annotated by some twenty specialists. (This list does not take into account the documents which are currently being processed.)

  • Asia
  • Europe

  • North America

The Neo-Caledonian materials represent a sample of the collection of over one hundred documents prepared by the specialists of Oceanic languages at the LACITO for the multimedia library of the Tjibaou Cultural Center in Noumea. We hope to make other documents from this unique collection available on the internet in the near future.
